What I Love To Do

How I spend my time away from work energizes me and bolsters my motivation. The travel bug bit me early on in life. Within the past couple of years I've gone from studying abroad in Australia, then touring most of Europe to now living in Chicago. While I travel, I love to document what I see by creating fun films to look back on. I am a huge Chicago sports fan and played basketball and volleyball growing up. Haiti is a particular country close to my heart, having made a mission trip there some time ago. We continue to support organizations providing aid to the country and plan to visit again, hopefully frequently.
