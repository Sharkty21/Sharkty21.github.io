Lifecycle Management

From initial product ideation to launch, I have managed products throughout their lifecycle. Similar to Application Lifecycle Management, Product lifecycle management divides its work into distinct stages and uses Agile methods like Scrum to deliver. There's conception based on customer requirements or research and development, an initial design that goes through a design verification testing process, a realization phase where a finished good is validated through a verified process with scale in mind, then finally serviced where the part is sold and quality is continously monitored.

Testing and Debugging

Based on customer requirements and performance and safety certifications, test plans must be written for both products and their manufacturing process. I have created, tested and adjusted designs accordingly based on governing specifications such as UL and IEC. Products have an elaborate test plan for continous quality assurance on both sold product but also future innovations. As functionality is added or form changed, tests are added to the test plan to ensure continuing quality assurance and certification.
